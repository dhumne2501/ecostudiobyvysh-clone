About Us

Eco Studio by Vysh is one of India’s most affordable zero-waste stores. Our quality is something that we have not compromised on at all!


Majority of the things we have on our site are handmade, giving employment to people all around in India. Our sourcing centres are heavily dependent on women. Giving a quality life and employment to women too.

Our packaging is entirely zero-waste too. We also re-use plastic wrapping that we collect from friends and family as they receive from Amazon or Flipkart. Nothing goes to waste with us. Be it a box or a packing label.

We truly believe in a circular economy and take extra steps to ensure you live your greenest life possible ✨

And trust us, as a brand, Eco Studio by Vysh truly believes that every single person can make a profound difference to the planet just by choosing what they eat and buy ✨🌻
